Analyst Agent

Ask your data a question.

The analyst agent retrieves relevant rows from your dataset, reasons over them, and returns a structured, evidence-backed answer.

How it works

  • You ask a business question in natural language.
  • The agent retrieves relevant dataset rows using semantic search.
  • A large language model reasons over the evidence.
  • Response includes summary, insights, patterns, and recommendations.

Sample question

Question: "Which visitor segments should we prioritize, and why?"

Summary
Returning visitors and users from referral/organic traffic sources show the highest commercial value, with conversion rates 3–4× higher than new/direct visitors. Targeting these segments with retention and high-intent campaigns would maximize ROI.
Key Insights
  • Returning visitors: 7.2% conversion vs. 2.1% for new visitors
  • Organic traffic: 6.5% conversion (highest ROI)
  • Referral traffic: 5.9% conversion (trusted sources)
  • Direct traffic: 3.2% conversion
  • Social traffic: 2.8% conversion (lowest performance)
Patterns
  • High intent correlation: users who visit multiple pages and spend >2 min convert at 8–12%
  • Bounce rate predictor: sessions with bounce rate >60% almost never convert
  • Page value signal: sessions >$5 page value show 15% conversion rate
  • Seasonal lift: November & December show 1.5× higher conversions than average months
Recommendations
  • Build a retention program focused on returning visitors — lowest CAC, highest LTV
  • Increase spend on organic + referral channels; reduce or optimize social spend
  • Create exit-intent campaigns for high-bounce sessions to recover abandonment
  • Segment by session page value — prioritize high-value sessions with higher discounts and faster delivery
  • Plan Q4 campaigns early — allocate budget to capture seasonal peak

Evaluation Pipeline

Systematic model comparison.

All four models and four prompt styles are evaluated on the same question set. Results measure keyword coverage, recommendation quality, structural completeness, and groundedness.

What's measured

  • keyword_score: Business terminology coverage
  • recommendation_score: Quality & usefulness of action items
  • completeness_score: Structural JSON field completion
  • groundedness_score: Answer overlap with retrieved evidence
