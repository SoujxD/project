Business insight narrative

  • Conversion rises when visitors show stronger intent through deeper product engagement.
  • Returning visitors and efficient traffic sources are the most attractive target customers.
  • Higher page value is a strong indicator of purchase readiness.
  • Lower bounce and exit rates align with better commercial outcomes.

Sample analyst questions

  • Which visitor segments and traffic sources should we prioritize?
  • What patterns distinguish converting sessions from non-converting sessions?
  • How does bounce rate affect conversion performance?
  • Which month appears strongest for revenue-oriented campaigns?

Evaluation metrics

  • keyword_score: how much expected business language appears in the answer.
  • recommendation_score: how actionable and complete the recommendations are.
  • completeness_score: whether the structured output fields are fully returned.
  • groundedness_score: how strongly the answer reflects retrieved dataset evidence.
